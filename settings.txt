SETTINGS
--------
Enter dates to be scraped (the more dates given, the more likely there will be a good screenshot) in the format YYYYMMDD separated by commas: 20210212,20210213,20210214
Screenshot width															    : 1920
Screenshot height															    : 3000
Time to wait after full page load before taking screenshot in milliseconds (the longer, the more accurate the screenshot will be)	    : 100000
Reload the page before the delay (it takes longer, but creates better results in most cases) (true/false)				    : true

URLS
----
From below the next separator, enter the URLs to scan in the format url::name, for example https://www.adobe.com/uk/::Adobe UK. Each URL be on a new line.
----
https://www.adobe.com/uk/::Adobe UK
https://www.adobe.com/it/::Adobe Italy
